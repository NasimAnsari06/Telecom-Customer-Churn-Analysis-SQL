# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn & Revenue Analytics (SQL)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior and revenue trends for a telecom company using SQL.

The objective is to identify high-risk customers, understand churn patterns, analyze revenue loss, and generate business insights that can help improve customer retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies lose significant revenue when customers leave their services.

This project answers key business questions:

- What is the overall churn rate?
- Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
- Which contract types have the highest churn?
- How much revenue is lost due to churn?
- Who are the highest-value customers?
- Which customers should be prioritized for retention?

## 💡 Business Insights

- Month-to-month customers show higher churn risk.
- Customers without support services are more likely to leave.
- High-value customers contribute a significant share of revenue.
- Customer retention efforts should focus on high-risk segments.
